Welcome From Valorant

This is Player Review

Valorant is one of those Counter Strike type of games but playing a group of preset characters with their unique abilities. I would say Valorant is pretty solid in terms of gunplay and balanced abilities, the abilities are the only new aspect of the game which are mostly about defending, distractions and front assault. It is one of those FTP games that doesn't requires high end specs to play the game which makes the game very approachable, the graphics are clean and a basic form with cartoonish look but that's what i like it, it is simple as i see nothing messy or detailed to see.

Nelson

It can mean many different things when someone says that the shooting, or action, in a game feels good, so what did Riot Games do with the gunplay in Valorant that makes it work so well? The guns — which players have to purchase at the beginning of each round, again like Counter-Strike — are weighty; they have aggressive recoil and spray bullets all over the screen if you don’t know what you’re doing.

Mary

Valorant’s main game mode is simple. Two teams of five players square off, with one team on offense and the other on defense. The teams switch places after 12 rounds, and the first team to win 13 rounds wins the match. The attacking team attempts to plant a bomb, called the Spike, at one of the bomb sites on the game’s four maps. The defending team has to stop them, either by killing everyone on the opposing side, or defusing the bomb once it has been planted. Each player only has one life per round, so either side can win simply by killing everyone else on the other team.
